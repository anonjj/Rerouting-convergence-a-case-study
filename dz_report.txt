Section VI-A insertion paragraph:
The higher d_z values for OLSR relative to AODV in Scenarios 1–3 reflect the lower variance of the per-run PDR difference under OLSR (baseline SD ≈ 0.15 pp vs. 0.68 pp for AODV), confirming that the tighter OLSR baseline amplifies the standardised effect size even when the raw mean difference is essentially identical across protocols. Formally, d_z = mean(PDR_GRAF_i − PDR_Baseline_i) / SD(PDR_GRAF_i − PDR_Baseline_i) for i = 1…20 paired runs sharing the same RNG seeds.
